About

From Marine Musician to software developer, I provide attention to detail and discipline for a focused mind. I decided to get into software development because I had previously began learning python and found that I really enjoyed coding. I then seeked out ways to set myself up for success and started teaching myself HTML and CSS. When I learned that with my GI Bill I could go to a coding bootcamp I began seeking out coding bootcamps, and to my amazement found Eleven Fifty. Now I am currently a student at Eleven Fifty Academy, pursuing a career that I have a passion for.

As a developer I want to create a product that gives the consumer ease-of-use and is visually appealing. Not only am I looking to do this but also implement my strengths of problem-solving, team work, attention to detail, and creativity.
